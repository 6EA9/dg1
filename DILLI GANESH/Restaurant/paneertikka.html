Paneer Tikka

Masters Of Kitchen

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made from chunks of paneer marinated in yogurt and spices, then grilled or roasted to perfection. Served with mint chutney, it’s a delight for vegetarians and a star in every feast.

Price: ₹120

Recipe Info

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Marinating Time: 1 hour

Total Time: 1 hour 35 minutes

Course: Appetizer

Cuisine: Indian

Servings: 4

Calories: 300 kcal (approx.)

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Skewers
  • Grill or oven
  • Basting brush
  • Serving plate

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 capsicum and 1 onion, cubed
