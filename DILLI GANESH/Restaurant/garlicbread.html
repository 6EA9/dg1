Garlic Bread Recipe

Masters Of Kitchen

This garlic bread is golden, crispy, buttery, and full of bold garlic flavor. Whether you're serving it with pasta, soup, or as a stand-alone snack, this homemade version is easy, versatile, and way better than store-bought. Includes cheesy and vegan variations!

Price: ₹50

Recipe Info

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Course: Appetizer

Cuisine: Italian

Servings: 6

Calories: 200 kcal (approx.)

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Spoon or spatula
  • Baking sheet
  • Foil (optional)
  • Serrated knife
  • Oven or broiler

Ingredients

  • 1 large loaf French or Italian bread
  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, minced (or 1 head roasted garlic)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (omit if using salted butter)
  • Optional: ½ cup shredded mozzarella or Parmesan
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning, or basil
