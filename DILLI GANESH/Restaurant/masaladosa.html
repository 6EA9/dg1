Masala Dosa

Masters Of Kitchen

A crispy and golden South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with a flavorful spiced potato masala. Served hot with coconut chutney and sambar, it's a beloved breakfast and dinner classic!

Recipe Info

Prep Time: 8 hours (includes soaking & fermentation)

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 8 hours 30 minutes

Course: Breakfast / Dinner

Cuisine: South Indian

Servings: 2

Calories: 250 kcal (approx.)

Equipment

  • Non-stick tawa or griddle
  • Spatula
  • Mixing bowls
  • Ladle
  • Blender or wet grinder

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Curry leaves
  • Fresh coriander (chopped)
