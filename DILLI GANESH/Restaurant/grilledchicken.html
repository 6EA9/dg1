Grilled Chicken with Rosemary

Tender and juicy grilled chicken marinated in a rosemary, garlic, and lemon blend. This flavorful recipe is perfect for summer grilling and weeknight dinners.

Recipe Info

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 2 hours 10 minutes

Course: Main Course

Cuisine: American, Mediterranean

Equipment

  • Grill (gas or charcoal)
  • Mixing bowl

  • Tongs
  • Meat thermometer

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts or thighs (boneless or bone-in)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
